Vision & Mission

To establish SIGCEAA as a multi-dimensional group which should keep generating learners and leaders in various fields extending to wider working sectors, localities and bear social responsibilities through Education, Research & Development, Innovation, Skill Development . Establish Alumni connects Globally across for connecting Alumni also within themselves and with SIGCEAA. To promote Alumni interests SIGCEAA shall grow, through creation of provisions for enough numbers of platforms extending from technology to cultural , financial and any upcoming sector .


Shall set the reputation of SIGCE ‘HIGH’ by getting an uncut edge through limitless inputs, improvisation of the same shall be accompanied by interactions, discussions and participation of SIGCEAA with industry, academicians or groups that bear social and commercial interest. To foster the culture of Research, Development, Innovation for various small and big solutions through channelising inputs and develop proper synergy between various stakeholders of the college.
